My Journey

Education, leadership, and milestones along the way.

2019 – 2020

SEE — Secondary Education

Shree Kalika Nepal Rastriya Secondary School

Completed the School Leaving Examination, building the foundational academic base before entering higher studies.

2020 – 2022

+2 Science

Omega College, Kumarpati

Studied Physical Science stream. Developed interest in engineering, physics, and problem-solving that eventually led to choosing Industrial Engineering.

2022

Joined Thapathali Campus, IOE

B.E. Industrial Engineering — Tribhuvan University

Began the journey in Industrial Engineering at the Institute of Engineering. Started engaging with student communities from the very first semester.

2023

Graphic Designer — GAP Thapathali

Regional Student Club (Gulmi, Arghakhanchi, Palpa)

Joined GAP Thapathali as a graphic designer. Contributed to event posters, social media content, and visual branding for the club.

2023

Joint Secretary — SOIES Nepal

Society of Industrial Engineering Students

Elected as Joint Secretary. Coordinated conferences, the Industrial Estate Research Program, Microsoft training, and published Industrial Vision. Organized the flagship Yathartha tech fest.

Early 2024

President — GAP Thapathali

Regional Student Club

Promoted to President. Led the "home far from home" initiative, connecting students from western Nepal studying at Thapathali Campus. Later resigned to take on SOIES presidency.

Aug 2024 – Aug 2025

President — SOIES Nepal

Society of Industrial Engineering Students, Nepal

Elected president. Organized 20+ events: Yathartha 3.0, National Olympiad, Ergo Reignite 2.0, Job Fair (MOU with Nebico), research workshops, debate competitions, IE promotional campaign "Beyond Machines," and more.

2025

NEC Research Grant — Wind Turbine

Supervised by Asst. Prof. Suman Acharya

Research proposal accepted by NEC on omnidirectional wind turbines — studying the impact of surface area and vent size changes. Presentation phase in progress.

Present

6th Semester — Building Forward

Thapathali Campus, IOE

Continuing academic studies, wrapping up presidential tenure, advancing research, and developing the E-Library and portfolio. Hamsafar — going where the wind takes me.
